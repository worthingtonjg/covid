OCI Mission

The mission of OCI is to treat widespread and deadly respitory diseases with our unique technology platform.

Summary

The OCI ILD Technology inhibits the CYTOKINE STORM the DEADLIEST ACTION of Inflammatory Lung Disease (ILD).

The KEY applications of the OCI ILD Technology are:

  • Covid
  • SARS
  • MERS
  • Inflammatory Asthma
  • Influenza A

The OCI ILD Technology is NOT antibody dependent, this is critical for the following reasons:

  • Patients that have been exposed to COVID-19 through vaccination or infection may not develop neutralizing antibodies which provide long-term immunity against the virus.
  • These patients may, however, develop recognizing antibodies, potentially exposing them to a more severe disease course, should they become infected in the future.
  • As the OCI ILD Technology is not antibody dependent, it is key to treating patients who do not develop neutralizing antibodies.
